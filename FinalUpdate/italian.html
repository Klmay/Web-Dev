Italian Cooking

Italy is a place many people dream of. It's also the first place people tend to think of when it comes to pasta and vice versa. But Italy is much more than pasta, and this class will teach you about the other things that come from there.

Italian cuisine tends to tie closely into Mediterranean cuisines, but with a few differences, one being that they use more cured meats rather than fresh. In this class you will be learning to make things such as:

  • Risotto
  • Gelato
  • Arancini
  • And, of course, different types of pasta

Whether you're wanting a simple meal that will please the whole family or are wanting to impress a date by making some classics, you'll find what you're looking for in this class!
