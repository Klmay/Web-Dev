Dress Code

Much like in an actual restaurant, you will need to follow a specific dress code during class. Violation of the dress code will bar you from class until you arrive in appropriate attire:

  • No long, loose sleeves
  • No open toe shoes
  • No heels
  • Students with long hair are required to tie it back
  • You will be provided a hair net and gloves
  • Students with long beards will be provided a beard net

Class Times

We offer classes every week at the same times. Keep in mind, each class must be paid for seperately. The fee for the class goes towards restocking supplies and ingriedients for the class in question. The building is closed Sundays to give our chefs time to clean and restock. We do not meet on Christmas or Thanksgiving, but we do offer classes prior to them with activities centered around them.
